[Day 136, 2 at night]
In Solitude, near the city gates with the player accompanied by Lydia, Serana, Frea, and a couple of chickens, Octieve San confronts Noster Eagle-Eye late at night, exclaiming in surprise and mild irritation, "What in Oblivion are you doing out here at this hour, you old beggar?"—marking the start of their roadside exchange.
