In a dimly lit stone chamber of a Solitude inn, Xander and Lydia engage in sharp banter over watery ale and the room's meager spread of cheese and apples; Xander mocks the Imperial polish while complimenting Lydia's battle-worn greatsword, prompting her to retort about his salt-crusted pirate reek and Forsworn kills for her Thane. The exchange escalates with mutual insults—Xander calling her a Nord broad with a sharp tongue, Lydia labeling him an Imperial fop whose ship sank due to poor sailing—before Xander clarifies his seafaring past, revealing he captained the Argent Raptor until it splintered on a cursed iceberg in the Sea of Ghosts.
