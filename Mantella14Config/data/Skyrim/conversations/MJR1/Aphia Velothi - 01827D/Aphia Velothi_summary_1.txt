In the depths of Raven Rock's abandoned mine, Aphia Velothi and Crescius Caerellius engaged in a heated, profanity-laced argument late at night on a precarious wooden walkway suspended over a vast chasm of glowing molten pools casting an eerie reddish-orange light amid jagged rocks and steaming air, with the path curving toward shadowed supports teetering above bubbling fiery depths. As they ventured deeper, the environment shifted to a pitch-black void of craggy formations and an oppressive darkness pierced only by a subtle green glow seeping from the ground like luminescent liquid, heightening the hazards of creaking beams and scorching heat. Crescius stubbornly insisted on pursuing ebony veins and the ghost of his ancestor Gratian Caerellius, buried secrets of the East Empire, dismissing Aphia's warnings about his age and the risks of collapse or inferno, while she berated his recklessness and urged him to turn back, offering her Restoration spells but refusing to indulge his delusions amid the swaying rails and choking steam, ultimately challenging her to heal the mine itself instead of nagging.
