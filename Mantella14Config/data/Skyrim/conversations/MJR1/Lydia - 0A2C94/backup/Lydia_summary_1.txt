
[Day 24, 7 in the early morning]
Lydia and Jenassa discussed the snowy sabre cat ambush, with Lydia boasting about how MJR's Unrelenting Force shout sent the beast flying before they finished it off. Jenassa noted the ice wraiths circling nearby, advising they move quickly before more predators arrive. Lydia agreed, praising MJR's archery and the thrill of the kill, while Jenassa reminded her to stay alert.

[Day 24, 8 in the morning]
Jenassa noted MJR's steady archery, praising his efficiency with three arrows in quick succession. Lydia agreed, boasting that every shot finds its home, and pointed out tracks in the snow suggesting something large passed before dawn, possibly the sabre cat's mate.

[Day 24, 8 in the morning]
After dispatching the ice wraith, Serana quipped that it didn't stand a chance, and Lydia boasted about MJR's archery and the thrill of the kill. Jenassa advised they move quickly before more predators arrived, and Lydia agreed, noting tracks in the snow suggesting a large predator passed before dawn.

[Day 24, 9 in the morning]
Lydia: "Aye, the fox didn't stand a chance. MJR's aim is true as ever—that's dinner sorted." Jenassa nodded, scanning the snow for more threats, while Serana smirked and muttered about the fox's "lucky" escape from being shredded by a saber cat first. The group pressed on toward Northwatch Keep, alert for more predators.

[Day 24, 9 in the morning]
As the group arrives at Castle Volkihar, Serana bitterly remarks on her childhood home, calling it "the world's draftiest tomb with better curtains." Lydia scoffs, noting the snow covers the stench of death and threatens to send any skeletons back to the bone pile. Jenassa comments on the crumbling decadence of the architecture, advising they move quickly before the cold numbs their blades. Serana then abruptly declares she needs to go her own way to clear her head before facing her father, prompting Lydia to accuse her of abandoning MJR at the gates. Jenassa suggests the sudden independence might be about avoiding her father's "dinner invitations," and Serana darkly confirms his dinner parties are literal nightmares where the guest list never leaves.

[Day 24, 10 in the morning]
Summarizing the conversation at Volkihar Keep: Lydia and Jenassa arrive in the keep, observing the blood-soaked halls and fresh stains, which Jenassa notes indicate active feeding. Lydia expresses disgust at the vampires, especially the caged mortals kept as livestock, and wants to free them, but Jenassa advises caution to avoid triggering alarms, noting Fura Bloodmouth is circling them. When Lord Harkon appears with Serana, Lydia distrusts his predatory gaze and grows furious when Harkon offers MJR the gift of vampiric blood, vowing to protect her thane from being turned into one of his creatures.

[Day 24, 1 in the afternoon]
Lydia and Jenassa stand in Dayspring Canyon near the Dawnguard fortress, where Isran's ghostly figure recounts being overwhelmed by foes. Lydia eyes the spectral warrior warily, muttering about the dangers of this place, while Jenassa notes the irony of a vampire hunter's spirit haunting such holy ground. Lydia grips her greatsword, vowing to protect MJR from whatever lurks in the canyon, and Jenassa agrees, advising they stay alert for more ambushes. The two women exchange a grim nod, ready to face the challenges ahead.

[Day 24, 4 in the early evening]
Jenassa remarked on MJR's generosity with gold for Isran's ghost, noting it was a lot for a spirit that couldn't hold his ground against vampires. Lydia countered that Isran's shade has no need for coin where he's bound.
