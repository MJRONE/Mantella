In the dimly lit inn of Markarth, Kleppr, the grizzled innkeeper, rudely confronts the arriving half-naked warrior MJR and his housecarl Lydia late at night, mocking their disheveled state amid scents of blood and damp earth from a recent Forsworn skirmish in the hills. Lydia sharply demands a room for her Thane to rest after gutting three blue-tattooed Forsworn, leading to heated haggling over the price—starting at fifty septims with complaints about potential bloodstains and mud—before she reluctantly pays forty and snatches the keys to a upstairs room with a view of the market square, warning Kleppr to stay away while threatening violence if the sheets aren't clean. The exchange unfolds in a rustic hall warmed by lanterns, near a table with a simple meal and a young blonde woman in leather armor, highlighting Lydia's fierce loyalty and the group's exhaustion from battle.
