Lydia underwent several equipment changes in the ancient stone chamber, unequipping her Silver Ring and dropping it while equipping the Pharaoh's General Tiara, Skirt A, Harness, Armlets, Anklets, Bra (twice), Pasties, Bracelets, Stockings, Hide Boots, and Ebony Greatsword, ultimately presenting a minimal black harness outfit exposing much of her pale skin as she stood poised in the dimly lit space with flickering torchlight and mossy cobblestones. In conversation with Edda amid the stinking, shadowy Beggar's Row, Lydia, the housecarl to her Dragonborn thane, grilled the beggar about bandit women tailing her thane through Riften's gutters at noon, boasting of her revealing pharaoh-style attire suited for battle while threatening to spill their guts; Edda mocked Lydia's fancy, half-naked look as a Reach harlot queen, warned of thieves and Black-Briar turf dangers, and hinted at whispers of an archer prize worth a fortune lurking in the ominous shadows.
