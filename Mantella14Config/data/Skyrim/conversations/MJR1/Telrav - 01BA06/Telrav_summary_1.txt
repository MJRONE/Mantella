[Day 11, 5 in the early morning]
In the rain-slicked ruins of Nilheim at dawn, Telrav lamented the storm's misery and promised gold for safe passage, with Jenassa grumbling about soaked leather but ready to fight for reward; upon arrival, they discovered a bloodied, unconscious woman in torn green rags, prompting Jenassa's suspicion and Telrav's feigned shock at bandit interference. Combat erupted as a bandit struck Jenassa with a steel mace, but MJR's Daedric arrow and accidental Unrelenting Force shout aided the group in slaying the foe—Lydia and Daegon nearby—leaving Jenassa searching for more threats amid the misty haze, rabbits, and a fox.
