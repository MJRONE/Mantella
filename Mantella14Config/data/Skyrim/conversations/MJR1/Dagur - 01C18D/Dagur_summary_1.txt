During the late-night conversation at the Frozen Hearth inn in Winterhold, Serana bantered with innkeeper Dagur about enduring the harsh blizzard, the merits of mead over spells for warmth, family life, and the Jarl's anti-magic prejudices, playfully suggesting spiking drinks with skooma to loosen up the locals and spark a magical free-for-all against Korir's rule, before toasting to a warmer future without chaos. Amid this, Serana equipped an elaborate Pharaoh's General outfit including tiara, stockings, skirt A, pasties, harness, bracelets, armlets, anklets, and bra (after briefly unequipping and re-equipping it), along with hide boots and a Dwarven mace; she rested on a bench before standing up, while MJR dropped Calcelmo's Stone Rubbing and picked up the Nightingale Blade.
