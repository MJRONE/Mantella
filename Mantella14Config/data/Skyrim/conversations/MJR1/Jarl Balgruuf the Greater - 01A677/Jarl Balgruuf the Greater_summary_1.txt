[Day 2, 3 at night]
In Dragonsreach's dimly lit hall, MJR informs Jarl Balgruuf of Helgen's destruction by a dragon, confirming Irileth's warnings; Balgruuf praises MJR's initiative, rewards him with a token of esteem, and enlists his aid for court wizard Farengar’s dragon investigations, as nearby guards, Jenassa, and Farengar observe amid the antique wooden surroundings.

[Day 8, 8 in the morning]
In Dragonsreach, Jarl Balgruuf rewards the Dragonborn (MJR) for retrieving the Dragonstone by allowing property purchase in Whiterun and gifting an item from his armory, then urges him to aid Irileth against the dragon at the Western Watchtower; MJR reveals absorbing dragon power, prompting Balgruuf to explain the Dragonborn's Thu'um gift and the Greybeards' summons at High Hrothgar, as nearby Whiterun Guards affirm readiness amid the hall's ancient wooden grandeur, with Nelkir sarcasically noting prophecies and the whispering door's secrets.
