[Day 12, 1 at night]
[Day 11, dawn in Ivarstead] Upon arriving in Ivarstead under a clear sky after Nilheim's rains, the Guard welcomed the group with praise for Dibella's starry blessing and pointed out Vilemyr Inn's warm glow for rest before the treacherous Seven Thousand Steps to snowy High Hrothgar, where Greybeards await the Dragonborn; Lydia echoed the sentiment, invoking Dibella's embrace after cleaving bandit sluts, vowing to guard her Thane MJR devotedly while itching for the climb amid nearby Jenassa, Daegon, Temba Wide-Arm, chickens, and cows.

[Day 12, 3 at night]
Under a starry Ivarstead night sky, the Guard praised Dibella's clear blessing after Nilheim's rains and inquired about bandit troubles en route, prompting Daegon to gripe about her soaked trek and Korpse's discomfort while boasting of ashing the foes; Lydia vowed unyielding protection for Thane MJR amid looming High Hrothgar. The Guard warned of skulking bandits and frost trolls on the Steps, with Riften guards vigilant; Daegon eagerly offered her coven-honed flames from Cyrodi against such threats, mocking Dibella's "pretty lies," drawing Lydia's scornful retort of "coven nonsense from elf witches?" nearby Jenassa, Gwilin, Klimmek, chickens, and an elk.
