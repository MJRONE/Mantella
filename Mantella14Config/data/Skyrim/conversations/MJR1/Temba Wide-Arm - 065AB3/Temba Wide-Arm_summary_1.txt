[Day 12, 2 at night]
In the Vilemyr Inn of Ivarstead late at night, Temba Wide-Arm banters flirtatiously with innkeeper Wilhelm about the reclusive Greybeards at High Hrothgar, bear troubles plaguing her mill, ghostly whispers from Shroud Hearth Barrow, and rumors of dragons, face-changers, and Dark Brotherhood activity from Riften and Windhelm.
