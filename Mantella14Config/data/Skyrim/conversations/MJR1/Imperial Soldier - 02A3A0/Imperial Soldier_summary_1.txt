In a misty, moonlit landscape near Shor's Stone, with dense forests and a cavern entrance nearby, Lydia, the housecarl and sworn shield to Thane MJR the Dragonborn, encounters an Imperial Soldier on patrol. The soldier crudely remarks on the clear night aiding bandit activity, teases Lydia about tailing the bounty-marked Dragonborn, and boasts of the Empire's watchful eyes amid fog-shrouded raider threats. Lydia retorts fiercely, affirming her loyalty and role, while the banter escalates into political jabs, with the soldier defending Imperial rule over Skyrim's "frozen shithole" and Lydia dismissing the emperor in favor of Dibella's glory.
