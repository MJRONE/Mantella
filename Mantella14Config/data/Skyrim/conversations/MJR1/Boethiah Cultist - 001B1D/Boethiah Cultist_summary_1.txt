In a snowy, starlit mountain landscape near a distant cabin and a glowing trail leading to a frozen stream, Serana provocatively discusses her skooma stash as a prelude to betrayal and ecstasy involving Boethiah, then probes the Boethiah Cultist for his secrets amid the frozen peaks. The cultist retorts by revealing Boethiah's whispers of using vampires like Serana as ideal sacrifices, threatening to carve out her secrets while noting how the snow muffles screams and the cabin's light mocks her eternal coldness.
