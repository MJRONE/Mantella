In the dimly lit chamber of Understone Keep in Markarth, Serana banters provocatively with a gruff City Guard who accuses her of trespassing and suspicious behavior at an unholy hour, labeling her a "pale-skinned harlot" and potential "bloodsucker" rifling through a ledger possibly tied to Jarl Igmund's bribes. Serana retorts with sharp wit, dismissing the charges as hypocritical while teasing about taming beasts, ancient carvings resembling monkey heads, Reachman influences, and the guard's role as a "tin-can rent-a-cop" for the Silver-Blood family, ultimately mocking the ledger as a "wet dream of Nord finances" linked to exploitative secrets behind an ornate door, keeping the exchange tense and flirtatious without resolution.
