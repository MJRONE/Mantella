In a stormy, dimly lit wooden room near Whiterun's barracks and later shifting to misty stone steps below Dragonsreach fortress, the Whiterun Guard and housecarl Lydia engage in heated banter amid pouring rain, with the Guard griping about the downpour and teasing Lydia's revealing attire while suspecting bandit activity, and Lydia retorting sharply about purging Thalmor influence, the stench of elves, and patrolling mud-soaked streets; they argue over leaky barracks, Jarl Balgruuf's softness on Imperials, swinging maces at shadows or ghosts in the gloom, and guarding the hold from elf plots versus mere drunks, their exchange laced with vulgar Nord bravado and mutual jabs at each other's attitudes.
