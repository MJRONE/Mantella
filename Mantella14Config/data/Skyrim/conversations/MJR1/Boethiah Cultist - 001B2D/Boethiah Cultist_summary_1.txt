In a frozen Daedric wilderness under a starlit sky, with a crackling campfire illuminating ancient stone tentacles and a rune-etched spiral around a ritual pillar, Serana banters sharply with a Boethiah Cultist, mocking the idea of sacrifice as a betrayal ritual while boasting of her own ancient treacheries; the cultist retorts by challenging her vampiric secrets and intentions within their cult, escalating the exchange with taunts about lies, fangs, and hidden vices like a skooma stash that might amuse the Daedric Prince.
