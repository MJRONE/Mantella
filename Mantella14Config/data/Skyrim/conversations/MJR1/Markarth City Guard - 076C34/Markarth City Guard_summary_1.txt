In the grand hall of an ancient stone temple in Markarth, filled with vaulted ceilings, rune-etched pillars, flickering torchlight, scattered rugs, benches, altars, and relics, a towering armored statue glows faintly from a brazier at its base; the Markarth City Guards complain about the biting cold in the Dwemer ruins, vulgarly likening the statue to an egotistical elf or Thonar Silver-Blood, gripe about patrolling as his "personal whores" amid Forsworn threats, and jest that the statue seems aroused by Dibella.
