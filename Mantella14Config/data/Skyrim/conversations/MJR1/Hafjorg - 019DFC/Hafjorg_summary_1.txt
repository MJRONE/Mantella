In the dimly lit underground Prison Blackreach, Hafjorg complains about the foul midday fog and damp smell, suggesting the Black-Briars buy out her operation to move it upstairs, while Frissa Black-Briar retorts with mockery about Maven's disinterest and teases Hafjorg about hiding secrets under her merchant guise tied to Elgrim. Frissa describes the prison as a profitable pit full of exploitable rats and offers Hafjorg a job amid her skepticism, leading to crude exchanges where Hafjorg demands a vial of glowing slime to avoid dwemer dangers, only for Frissa to refuse and threaten her with it.
