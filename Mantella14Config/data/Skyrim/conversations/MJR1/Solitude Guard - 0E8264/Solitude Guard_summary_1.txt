In the dimly lit corridors of Solitude's Blue Palace at 2 a.m., a hostile Solitude Guard confronts Lydia, the Whiterun housecarl guarding her Thane the Dragonborn, accusing her of sneaking around like a brute eyeing the Jarl's throne or silverware amid red banners and empty throne rooms. Lydia retorts sharply, defending their legitimate Dragonborn business and warning of a glowing red tripwire blocking the path ahead in the narrow, oppressive hallway with wooden paneling and worn carpets, while the guard dismisses it as mere security against spies and intruders.
