In a dimly lit wooden chamber of Fanari Strong-Voice's greathall, filled with the scent of aged timber and flickering candlelight, Serana banters provocatively with the Skaal leader, likening the space to her clan's crypt orgies and teasing about poisons like mead or skooma, while Fanari rebuffs her with pride in the All-Maker's clean gifts of air, water, and honest work, quickly detecting and confronting Serana's vampire scent as an outsider's rot and leech-like threat to their balanced life. The exchange escalates with Serana's mocking jabs at the Skaal's noble poverty in the frozen north and flirtatious threats about Fanari's jawline, met by Fanari's defiant boasts of felling reavers with her mace and warnings against bloodsuckers dreaming of empires.
