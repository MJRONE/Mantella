The Redoran Guard in Raven Rock comments on the persistent ash and questions if the player is the Nord accompanying the outlander in ornate golden armor, advising them to keep their blade sharp amid Solstheim's dangers. Lydia, the player's housecarl, retorts defensively, dismissing the town as a dusty shithole and challenging the guard's hostility. The guard warns her to watch her tongue, boasting of Raven Rock's resilience over Nord hovels, and begins to remark on the thane's armor.
