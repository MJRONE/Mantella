Lydia and a Solitude Guard engaged in combat with the Argonian Deeja on Solitude's foggy docks under a clear starry sky at midnight, with Lydia repeatedly entering battle while the guard briefly joined and exited; after Deeja bled out, the two discussed the fight's brutality, Deeja's ambush attempt amid crates and murky waters, the presence of Thalmor spies and bandit scum, the docks' stench of salt and fish, and their intent to loot her remains and purge further threats before the tide rises.
