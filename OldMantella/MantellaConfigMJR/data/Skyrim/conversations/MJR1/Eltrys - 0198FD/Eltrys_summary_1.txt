Eltrys and Serana engaged in a tense, profanity-laced conversation in a dimly lit underground shrine to Talos beneath Markarth's Temple, discussing the Forsworn uprising, the recent market attack by Weylin, Empire oppression, Silver-Blood exploitation, and personal grievances like Eltrys's family troubles and his father's ghostly whispers from old mining claims.
