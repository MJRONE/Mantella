Two Stormcloak soldiers banter crudely around a crackling campfire in a snowy pine forest under a vibrant purple aurora, mocking each other's attire and mead habits while toasting to retaking Solitude and bashing Imperial foes at dawn. They reminisce about a past skirmish near Windhelm where they gutted scouts in deep drifts, eye the misty cliffs and howling wolves in a nearby valley hinting at a hidden cave, and plan to scout an Imperial outpost in Haafingar amid the freezing wilderness.
