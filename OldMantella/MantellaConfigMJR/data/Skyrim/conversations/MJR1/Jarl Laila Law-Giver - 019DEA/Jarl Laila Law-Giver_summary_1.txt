In the grand hall of Windhelm's Palace of the Kings, amid the remnants of a feast under torchlight and chandeliers, Jarl Laila Law-Giver vents her frustration over her exile from Riften, now controlled by Maven Black-Briar and rife with skooma, thieves, and Imperial influences, while praising Ulfric's Stormcloak resistance against Imperials and Thalmor; Lydia echoes her disdain, vowing her Dragonborn thane's arrow will end Maven's rule and affirming loyalty to Nord causes.
