In the dimly lit Bannered Mare during the early morning chill, beggar Brenuin banters crudely with housecarl Lydia, boasting about teaching orphan Lucia to beg while seeking scraps and warmth by the hearth and barrels; Lydia, fiercely loyal to her Thane MJR the Dragonborn—who she says is out sniping bandits with his Daedric bow—threatens Brenuin with her Ebony Greatsword, dismissing him as a scheming Redguard fraud polluting Whiterun's Nord blood and warning him away from her charge, amid escalating insults referencing market scams, Hulda's upstairs presence, and even Serana trailing the Thane.
