In a heated exchange inside a warmly lit wooden longhouse and later in an adjacent dusk-lit outdoor courtyard at an outpost adorned with festive wreaths, Serana, a provocative blonde vampire in revealing black leather armor that later leaves her nearly bare, taunts the zealous Vigilant of Stendarr with vulgar innuendos about her desire for the Dragonborn MJR, skooma, and shared blood, while mocking his faith and midday sermons. The Vigilant retorts with fierce insults, branding her a blasphemous bloodsucker and undead whore, threatening to smash her with his mace under Stendarr's purifying light and Dibella's corrupted influence, escalating tensions without resolution as the solstice vigil looms.
