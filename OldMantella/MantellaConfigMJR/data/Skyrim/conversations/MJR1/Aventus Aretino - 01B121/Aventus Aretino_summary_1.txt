In a dimly lit orphanage room at three in the morning, Aventus Aretino and the vampire Serana whisper conspiratorially about murdering the snoring orphanage matron Grelod, with Aventus expressing eagerness for her death and revealing his awareness of Serana's vampiric nature, while Serana teases a bloodthirsty plan to end Grelod's tyranny, all amid crude jokes about the creaky bunks, dying fire, and the inviting bed where Serana lounges provocatively.
