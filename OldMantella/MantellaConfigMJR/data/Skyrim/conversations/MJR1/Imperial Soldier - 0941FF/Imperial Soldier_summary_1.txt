In the Palace of the Kings' grand dining hall at 4 AM, Serana and the Imperial Soldier infiltrate the Stormcloak stronghold amid a lavish feast of roasted meats, bread, and ale, where cloaked figures eat and converse under torchlight and chandeliers. Serana mocks the opulent space as Ulfric's ego-driven throne room and the quiet hour as deader than her family reunions, while the Soldier compares their stealthy entry to foxes in a henhouse, warning against loose talk that could turn them into the main course. They observe the Stormcloaks as drunk on false victory, with Serana quipping it's more like a last supper before the Empire's reckoning.
