In a secluded northern village bathed in the ethereal glow of dancing auroras over snowy peaks and frost-laden birch trees, Lydia, a fierce Nord housecarl, crudely admires the night sky's lights while decrying Riften's under the moon, then confronts patrolling Imperial Soldiers for intruding on Nord territory at three in the morning; the soldiers retort aggressively, accusing her of being Stormcloak-aligned and mocking her appearance amid the tense, lantern-lit standoff on creaking boardwalks surrounded by rustic cabins and scattered firewood.
