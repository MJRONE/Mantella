In the dimly lit, snow-dusted courtyard of Windhelm under swirling green auroras, Lydia, a fierce Nord housecarl loyal to the Stormcloaks, banters heatedly with a beaten Imperial soldier—a pale woman in a skimpy, tattooed outfit—who sits frostbitten on the ground after being roughed up by Ulfric's men behind a nearby ajar door. Their exchange escalates with crude insults about the Empire's crumbling hold, Thalmor spies, and Windhelm's enduring stone walls, revealing Lydia's unyielding Stormcloak pride and the soldier's defiant loyalty amid her vulnerable state, while the flickering torch casts warm light on the tense standoff.
