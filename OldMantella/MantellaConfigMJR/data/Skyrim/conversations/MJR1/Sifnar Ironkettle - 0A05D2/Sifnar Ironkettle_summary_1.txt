In the dimly lit stone corridor of the palace, amid flickering torchlight and tense echoes of dripping water, Captain Janel Gale urgently warns MJR of a killer in Werneth, urging a warning be sent, while an arched doorway beckons as an exit. Lydia confronts Sifnar Ironkettle about rumors of murders in the palace shadows and snowy outskirts, where victims are left gutted like horkers; Sifnar shares whispers of a mad butcher, and Lydia vows to protect her thane, referencing a bloody amulet etched with bones found by the captain, heightening the drafty halls' shiver of dread.
