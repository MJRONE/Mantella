In a dimly lit dungeon cell beneath Solitude, prisoner Bjartur, a fiery Stormcloak, banters with Lydia, the housecarl of the Dragonborn, who affirms her Nord loyalty and disdain for Imperials and Thalmor; amid flickering torchlight and scattered wooden debris near a shadowed arched door, Bjartur presses for escape, eager for a key or smash to freedom while mocking the oppressive gloom contrasting the sunny streets above, with Lydia reassuring their shared resolve against the Empire before Ahtar's potential axe falls.
