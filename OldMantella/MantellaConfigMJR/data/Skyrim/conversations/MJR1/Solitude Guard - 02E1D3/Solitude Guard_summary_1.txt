Lydia equips a full Pharaoh's General outfit including tiara, skirt, pasties, harness, bracelets, anklets, bra, and armlets, along with Hide Boots and an Ebony Greatsword, while ethereal UI panels display MJR's stats (health 85/100, stamina 60/80, mana 15/50, encumbrance 12%) and active quests like retrieving Levern's axe, delivering a bounty, returning tools, discussing adoption with Foreman Michel, appraising a factotum artifact, and transporting a dagger.
