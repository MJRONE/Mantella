The Whiterun Guards, recovering from a tense skirmish with the vampire Serana whose glowing eyes haunted them in the torchlight, banter crudely about the mocking starry sky and blood-red moon hanging low over ancient ruins, while spotting four slender blonde women approaching along a cobblestone path—bare-skinned and graceful, clad only in fur leg wraps, carrying packs and weapons, their forms illuminated by flickering torches amid dry shrubs and crumbling arches.
