In a tense confrontation within the dimly lit, mossy stone chambers of Mistwatch at three in the morning, Lydia fiercely defended her Thane MJR against the taunting Mistwatch Bandit, exchanging vulgar threats involving ebony blades, elven arrows, Dibella's wrath, and graphic dismemberment amid barrels, lanterns, and arched shadows, escalating without resolution as the bandit mocked her Nord loyalty and she vowed to gut him and his cloaked allies. During this exchange, Lydia repeatedly equipped and unequipped items including her Silver Ring, Hide Boots, Ebony Greatsword, and various Pharaoh's General attire pieces like the Bra, Anklets, Armlets, Bracelets, Harness, Pasties, Skirt A, Stockings, and Tiara, while MJR picked up and dropped Fjola's Wedding Band and took a Lesser Soul Gem.
