In the shadowed Solitude ruins under a starry night sky, the Imperial Soldier and Lydia engage in heated banter, with the Soldier griping about the mossy, bandit-infested stones and mocking Nords' heroism without Imperial legions, while Lydia retorts by calling Imperials Thalmor whores and vowing to protect Thane MJR from lurking bandits using Dibella's guidance.
