A Whiterun Guard and Lydia converse under a vivid aurora borealis at Whitewatch Tower, with the guard crudely marveling at the lights as potential divine signs or omens amid Skyrim's civil unrest, prompting Lydia to dismiss Imperial influences and hail the display as Dibella's fiery blessing urging Nords to embrace Stormcloak fervor and expel foreigners like Thalmor and Orcs; the guard rebukes her heresy toward Jarl Balgruuf's neutrality but concedes the aurora's inspiration, proposing they target bandits in the hills, to which Lydia questions his reference to "dragon shit."
