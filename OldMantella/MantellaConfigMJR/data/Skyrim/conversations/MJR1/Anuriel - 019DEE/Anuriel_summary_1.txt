Anuriel and Lydia engaged in a heated, vulgar exchange in the dimly lit halls and feast chamber of Windhelm's palace, griping about Maven Black-Briar's control over Riften, Ulfric Stormcloak's leadership, and the ongoing civil war, while toasting with mead amid cold stew, flickering embers, and banners bearing the rearing horse symbol; Anuriel confessed her past corruption by Maven's gold in the Thieves Guild, and Lydia defended Nord loyalty to the Dragonborn (MJR) and Dibella's favor, wrapping up with a toast to buried secrets and reclaiming Riften without resolution or departure. Throughout, Lydia repeatedly equipped and unequipped elements of the Pharaoh's General outfit—including the tiara, skirt A, harness, armlets, bra (twice), stockings, bracelets, pasties, anklets—along with Hide Boots, Ebony Greatsword, and a Silver Ring she picked up and later dropped, amid visual contexts of stone chambers, arched doorways, smoldering fireplaces, and a laden wooden table surrounded by hooded figures.
