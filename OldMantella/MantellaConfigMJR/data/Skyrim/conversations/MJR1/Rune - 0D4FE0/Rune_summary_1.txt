In the dimly lit underground chamber of Riften's Ratway at four in the morning, Serana banters crudely with Rune about the dismal surroundings, fantasizing about illicit thrills and probing for Thieves Guild secrets, while Rune teases her vampiric allure and deflects with jokes about seducing vermin or turning the sewers into a chaotic den. Serana presses on the Guild's ethics, questioning if they truly share spoils with Riften's beggars or hoard wealth selfishly, prompting Rune to begin responding skeptically on the notion of sharing.
