Gabriella and Lydia trade crude insults and barbs in a dimly lit cave, with Lydia explaining their visit on behalf of the Dragonborn to pursue a shadowy contract amid suspicious swords and relics.

In a dimly lit cavern heavy with the scent of damp earth and charred herbs, Gabriella, a robed Dark Elf figure, taunts Lydia about a glowing violet relic tied to the Night Mother and Sithis, mocking Nord reverence for Dibella and questioning the Thane's shadowy contract motives. Lydia fires back defensively, praising Dibella as a goddess of beauty and desire while deriding Sithis as a weak specter, and demands details on the relic amid complaints about the early hour and tomb conditions. Their banter escalates with mutual insults—Gabriella calling Dibella a harlot of empty promises, and Lydia labeling Gabriella a knife-eared whore in funeral rags—hinting at underlying tensions in their assassin-related alliance, while a separate rift-induced vision briefly overlays a topless woman in an Aldmeri beach-like forest fragment.
