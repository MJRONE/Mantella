In a dimly lit cavern pulsing with ethereal blue and violet glows from the Ebonmere pool and scattered luminous pools, Serana banters crudely with Nocturnal about the Daedric entity's shadowy domain, the stolen Skeleton Key pilfered by a thief named Frey, and the ancient directive etched on the walls to return it to the flowers on the Pilgrim's Path to restore luck to Skyrim's thieves. Their flirtatious, mocking exchange reveals Nocturnal's role as patron of thieves and Serana's vampiric intrigue tied to her companion MJR, culminating as the topic wraps with Nocturnal urging the key's return for potential fortune amid the charged, mysterious atmosphere; during this, MJR drops the Skeleton Key.
