The Whiterun Guards, enduring the harsh sun and heat near the White River ford, gossip crudely about a red-haired woman spotted earlier, suspecting her of attempting to steal a horse from the stables, possibly as a Stormcloak sympathizer or Forsworn scout from the Reach, with ties to shady activities around Riverwood's mill and an Argonian trader eyeing merchant carts; they dismiss some threats while bantering about her appearance and their discomfort in heavy armor.
