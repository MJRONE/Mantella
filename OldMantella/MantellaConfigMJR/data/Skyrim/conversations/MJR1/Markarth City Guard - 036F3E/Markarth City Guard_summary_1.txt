In the shadowed outskirts of Markarth, a belligerent city guard halts the Dragonborn Thane and his housecarl Lydia at the gates late at night, mocking their appearance and demanding their business under Jarl Igmund's orders while threatening arrest. Lydia sharply defends her Thane, identifying herself as his protector from Forsworn and bandits, and retorts with vulgar insults against the guard's allegiance to the Silver-Blood family, demanding passage for official Jarl business and threatening violence if obstructed.
