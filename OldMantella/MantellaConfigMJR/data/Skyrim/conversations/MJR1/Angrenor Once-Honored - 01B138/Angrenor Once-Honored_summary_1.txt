In a dimly lit ancient stone chamber under an aurora-lit sky, with frost and debris scattered about, Serana mocks the rundown Aretino residence as a "cozy little shithole" where ghosts might seek warmth, then questions an old man about squatting there to avoid the snow or awaiting the Jarl's charity; Angrenor Once-Honored retorts defensively, claiming the place as his by right of friendship and rejecting the insult.
