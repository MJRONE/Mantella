In this conversation, DiMA and Naveen discuss life at Acadia and the challenges they face as synths in the harsh world of the Commonwealth. DiMA emphasizes the importance of Acadia as a safe haven for synths and the need to bear the burden of secrets to protect their way of life. Naveen admits to struggling with the adjustment and the weight of their responsibilities, but ultimately agrees with DiMA that they must remain strong and united for the sake of their fellow synths.
