Miranda shared her memories of the Commonwealth before the apocalypse, describing it as more civilized and complex than Acadia. She mentioned that people there had their own beliefs and values, and while some were accepting of synths, others were not. Miranda worked as part of a medical group in a small settlement near Diamond City. The conversation shifted to the present, with both Miranda and Cog agreeing to focus on making Acadia a better place. They discussed the possibility of returning to the Commonwealth one day to see how it has changed, but for now, they are content to make the most of their current situation.
