The Child of Atom, Sister Harriet, encounters the player late at night. She introduces herself as one of the Children of Atom and invites the player to join them and be cleansed of their sins. The player requests caps, to which Sister Harriet agrees as she recognizes the need for financial resources. The player then asks her to bend over, and she willingly complies, grateful for the opportunity to serve and hoping for a blessing from the almighty Atom.

Piper engages in a flirtatious conversation with a Child of Atom, discussing the "pleasures" of embracing the glow of radiation. Despite the Child of Atom's warnings of temptation and damnation, Piper remains lighthearted and suggests comparing notes on pleasure later in private. However, the conversation turns serious when Piper mentions the dangers of radiation, and the need to find Rad-Away. The Child of Atom insists radiation is a blessing, but Piper dismisses this, saying she prefers enlightenment without the side effects. The two decide to set aside their religious debate and focus on the task at hand.

The Child of Atom greets the traveler, asking what brings them to the Nucleus in search of enlightenment and if they desire to be free of worldly desires and sins while embracing Atom's glow. The Archemist responds, explaining that following Atom's path is not easy, as it demands dedication, devotion, and the willingness to accept internal and external change. They state that Atom's love is all-encompassing, and through His divine glow, one can find true purpose and meaning in the post-apocalyptic world.
