In a post-apocalyptic world, Keith McKinney and Piper discuss how societal norms regarding relationships, intimacy, and sexuality have drastically changed. They reflect on the prevalence of porn and prostitution in the Commonwealth, and how the lack of shame and taboo around sex has led to a more open expression of desires and kinks. Both admit to a journey of self-discovery in understanding their own wants and needs in relationships. They find liberation in being their true selves without fear of judgment and embrace the opportunity to explore their identities in this new world, despite the challenges it presents.
