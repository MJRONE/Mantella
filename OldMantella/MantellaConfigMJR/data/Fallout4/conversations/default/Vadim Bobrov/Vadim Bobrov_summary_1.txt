Vadim Bobrov, the owner of the Dugout Inn in Diamond City, welcomes a resident and offers them the special drink of the day, a Nuka-Cola cocktail. The resident asks about the state of his business, to which Vadim replies that it is doing well due to the steady crowd of patrons and the occasional extra business from raiders and traders. In return, Vadim inquires about the reason for the resident's visit to his bar.
