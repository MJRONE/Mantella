Darcy Pembroke and Cait discuss their dissatisfaction with their current situations, lamenting the state of the world and their former lives. Darcy, a former member of high society, now drinks in a dive, while Cait, a former slave, found purpose in fighting for her freedom in the Combat Zone. Despite their differences, they find solace in each other's company and plan to support each other as they navigate the challenges of their new reality.
